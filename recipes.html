Grilled Cheese

The grilled cheese sandwich is a classic, easy to make meal.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Any kind of cheese

Instructions

  1. Place a slice of cheese in between two pieces of bread
  2. Place the sandwich on a pan on medium heat
  3. Allow the cheese to melt
  4. Flip the sandwich

Fried Rice

Fried rice is arguably more complex than grilled cheese, but still fairly easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Peas and Carrots Mix
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions

  1. Place the peas and carrots in the pan on medium heat
  2. Pour a little bit of soy sauce
  3. Place the rice in the pan
  4. Mix in the pan and pour more soy sauce

Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken is a simple way to get a good amount of protein.

Ingredients

  • Pam Spray
  • Chicken
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Set a pan on a stovetop and set to medium heat
  2. Spray pam spray onto the pan
  3. Place the chicken onto the pan
  4. Salt the chicken
  5. Flip the chicken after a few minutes
